Who I Am

I am Gideon Tetteh Luotey — a multidisciplinary technologist based in Ghana, whose work lives at the rare convergence of code, data, and design. My passion is not merely building things; it is building things that matter, that endure, that provoke. Walk with me.

With expertise spanning full-stack web development, data science & machine learning, graphic design, video editing, and AI engineering, I bring a holistic perspective to every project that I touch.
